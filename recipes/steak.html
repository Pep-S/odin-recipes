Reverse Seared Steak

Steak is great, but reverse searing elevates it to excellence. It is the process of cooking the steak in an oven on low heat first before finishing it up on a hot grill or pan for the sear.

Find the steps to create this masterpiece below:

Ingredients:

  • A nice Ribeye steak, at least 1.5 inches thick
  • Sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • MSG
  • High smokepoint oil like Corn Oil

Process:

  1. Season the room temperature steaks thoroughly
  2. Pre-heat the oven to 110 degrees Celsius
  3. Leave steaks in until the internal temperature reaches X for rare
  4. Completely pat dry the steaks, then lightly coat the steaks with oil
  5. On a high heat grill, sear each side of the steak for 1-2 minutes
  6. Let rest for 5-10 minutes, and serve
